Introduction

This web mainly focus on predicting the price of agricultural products. The results of the predictions can help people such as farmers to know the price in the future.

Function

1. Information:
You can get the data of agricultural products.

2. Price:
You can get the line chart with price that is predicted from test data. Besides, you can obtain the predicted information of agricultural products such as price, average deviation, standard deviation etc.

3. Volume:
You can get the line chart with volume that is predicted from test data. Besides, you can obtain the predicted information of agricultural products such as volume, average deviation, standard deviation etc.

4. Price trend:
You can get the line chart with a price trend that is predicted from test data. Besides, you can obtain the predicted trend information of agricultural products such as price,average deviation,standard deviation etc..
